Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

GharSet collects the following information to provide household admin services:

  • Account data: Email address, display name, and Google account ID if you sign in with Google.
  • Household data: Reminders, documents, family member names, and subscription preferences you create within the app.
  • Device data: Android advertising ID (for serving ads via Google AdMob).
  • Usage data: Anonymous app interaction data via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To sync your reminders, documents, and family data across devices via Firebase.
  • To serve contextual ads via Google AdMob (free tier only).
  • To process payments via Razorpay (premium subscriptions). Razorpay handles your payment data — we never see your card details.
  • To improve app stability with Firebase Crashlytics.

3. Data Storage & Security

All household data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore) and Firebase Storage. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We implement tamper detection to protect against modified APK installations.

4. Third-Party Services

GharSet uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Firestore database, Storage, Analytics, Crashlytics, Cloud Messaging
  • Google AdMob: In-app advertising
  • Razorpay: Payment processing for premium subscriptions
  • Google ML Kit: On-device OCR for document scanning (no data leaves your device)
  • Google Sign-In: Optional authentication method

5. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing support@gharset.app. We will process your request within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

GharSet is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

Email: support@gharset.app
